Magic Eye Alignment System (1.0)

Credit: Greg Pace 04/18/2022)


Part 1 - Line up regularly to the hole to get a good starting point.


Part 2 - Go cross-eyed until you can visualize the (left eye ball) right near the (right eye cup) and make alignment like if it was a 1-foot tap in. *Tip: This works best to start on short to medium putts, it will take a trained eye and lots of focus to make this work for long putts.


Part 3 - Visualize the return of the (left eye ball and club) to a standard view and tilt head position to make adjustments to have a smooth coming back in. Also visualize your target line with the two clubfaces square right before returning to a regular view.


Part 4 - Hit your line.
